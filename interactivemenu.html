Nadeshiko Kagamihara

Nadeshiko Kagamihara is a young girl from Nanbucho.
She is very cheerful and energetic, but easily forgets things.
She also likes to eat a lot.
(Source: Yuru Camp Wiki)

Rin Shima

Rin is a young girl who enjoys camping.
She usually camps by herself, until she meets Nadeshiko.
Rin has purple eyes, dark-purple hair, and pale skin.
(Source: Yuru Camp Wiki)

Aoi Inuyama

Aoi Inuyama is a young girl from the show Yuru Camp.
She is the co-president of The Outdoor Activities Club at her school alongside Chiaki Oogaki.
Aoi has medium-length brown hair, olive-green eyes, and pale skin.
(Source: Yuru Camp Wiki)

Chiaki Oogaki
