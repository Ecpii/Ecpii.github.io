T-Spins

Continual B2B2B2B2B2B2B pressure...

It was mentioned before in multi that T-Spins were an efficient way of building and sending garbage to your opponent quickly. This makes for a great and efficient attacking strategy of continually building T-Spins to pressure your opponent to the top.

Unlike 4-Wide, there is not a set pattern you need to follow; the major ideas are just to keep your B2B chain so that every T-Spin you clear sends more and more garbage, and to keep sending either Quads or T-Spins.
